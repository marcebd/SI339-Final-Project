🌟 The Story

Long ago, in the vibrant land of Webaria, Princess Semantica ensured all websites were readable, navigable, and joyful for every citizen — whether they used a mouse, keyboard, or screen reader.

But one day, the evil villain Anti-A11y (Browser’s distant cousin) kidnapped her and flooded the kingdom with inaccessible UI traps!

Only you — the hero of tab, contrast, and alt text — can defeat him.
